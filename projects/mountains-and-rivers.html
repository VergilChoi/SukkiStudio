Mountains and Rivers
----------------------------------------------
Branding Design
keywords:
Travel / Landscape / Chinese wind / movable type printing

This is a set of brand visual design for travel and cuisine. The brand's visual inspiration comes from last year's travel experience in Tibet, along the way I experienced the magic and changing nature landscape. I think when people stand in front of the mountain and the sea is very small, and feel relieved. Then travel will become a way of life in the future, I guess the future everyone is not necessarily living in a fixed place. Each person can choose a different period to live in different areas and experience different cultures and cuisines. So I put this concept into the brand. I combined with the undergraduate cultural and creative products and visual design reorganization and update to form a set of Chinese culture brand image and vi design.

In the production I selected the works of Wu Guamountains-and-riversnzhong and Lin Fengmian, their painting style is a combination of Chinese and Western, I learned in the color of Wu Guanzhong color ink. Orange and yellow can make people feel vitality. I also studied Kandinsky's work. He was the founder of the abstraction. He used dot, line, and face elements to express the lively and irregular character. On the other hand, in the Chinese elements of the wind, I studied the movable type printing technology, which is one of China's four major inventions, the benefits of this printing is the flexibility to allow different combinations of words printed together, and China Of the text have in common, the use of different radicals to form a Chinese characters. Therefore, I used to design the first radical and then spliced ​​together to design fonts, is a new attempt.
This project is not just about designing a brand's vision. But also I explore the culture, art and design before the relationship between the process. Through the combination of movable type and font design, you can find a new design method, in the future there will be more of this attempt to wait for us to explore.
