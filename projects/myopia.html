Myopia
----------------------------------------------
Manual book and font design
keywords:
Myopia / vertigo / astigmatism / pain

China is one of the countries with the highest incidence of myopia in the world, which inspired me to do a project to demonstrate the vision of myopia. The project to explore the process and experience of myopia, so that we can more intuitive understanding of the pain after myopia.

The entire book changes from clear to vague and from white to black which represent blindness. A variety of twisted forms in the book convey the feelings of myopia in an exaggeration way.
