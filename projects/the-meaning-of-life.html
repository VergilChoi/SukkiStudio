The Meaning of Life
----------------------------------------------
Symbol and illustration
keywords:
Self-awareness / Life form / Graduation

I observe that many students do not know what they want to do after graduation. I think this is and people's awareness of self-related? Perhaps the lack of meaning of life thinking, lack of self-awareness. The theme of the project is "the meaning of life." I hope that young people understand the forms of life and inanimate bodies, and inspire them to think about the meaning of existence between human beings and nature and help them understand the meaning of life more deeply. To make people with a better attitude to the purpose of life. These symbols and illustrations show in the streets, campuses, network environment, with the story of the text to the viewer to bring the diversity of thinking and inspiration.

Life can represent the form of life: the living body and non-living body. The key words of life are eggs, curled up, split, reorganized, I will be rounded into their shape and arrangement of such a combination. Then, I tried the macro shooting and production of "life" device. I went to the park to find some of the leaves and branches, leaves are a symbol of life, from birth to growth, until the old. I chose to use foam and soft lines to express life. Finally, I tried a white paint, turn them into white, this process is a process of life fading.
I hope that through such a project to inspire people to reflect on the meaning of their own life, for what you exist? But also for what to do now these things.
