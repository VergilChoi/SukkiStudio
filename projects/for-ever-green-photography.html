For-Ever-Green Photography
----------------------------------------------
documentry photography / publication
keywords:
Vegetation ／ Exotic Garden / Artificial Ecosystem

This photography publication aims to use photo archives to present the artificial illusion of high-rise garden through the glass reflections. The image shows the conflict of nature and architecture.

As green lifestyle rise around the world, people turn vegetation into products. The semiotic of plants has changed gradually. How the plants use to create health ideal lifestyle can be investigated in the future. On the other hand, this constructed health lifestyle images are enjoyed by their audience which can be consider as a type of manipulation. A developer can use plants as a tools to create a healthy illusion. A country, of course, can use the same way to generate positive impression too.
