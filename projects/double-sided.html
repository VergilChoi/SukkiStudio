Double-Sided
----------------------------------------------
Packaging Design
KEYWORDS:
Two sides / music / eyes / jazz

This project is for the jazz fans and do the double-sided character album. I collected a few songs from Billie Holiday to make a new album called "Come Rain Or Come Shine", the theme meaning everyone has a gloomy and sunny side, including the singer I like her own experience Is very legendary, so I borrowed her music and spirit to make the packaging, so that music becomes an emotional expression of the way of life. Through a series of double-sided visual design to make the album more humane.

I surveyed the album cover creator Alex Steinweiss visual works and Andy Warhol designed singer's album works. I selected rays, curves, and dots to express her personal characteristics. Ray on behalf of her appearance distributed the unique temperament, the sun and cheerful. Curves and points represent the rainy day, passing melancholy and sadness. From Billie Holiday's album color to extract the blue, black and gold, constitute a new album visual design.
